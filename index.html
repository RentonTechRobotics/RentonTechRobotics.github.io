What We Do

The RTC Robotics Club is dedicated to fostering a learning environment where students explore the exciting world of robotics and mechatronics. Our mission is to provide hands-on experience in designing, building, and programming robots. We welcome members of all skill levels to collaborate on both individual and team-based projects, allowing everyone to develop their skills while working on real-world applications.
